Sentiment Analyser | About Project

This project addresses the problem of sentiment analysis of a given text or tweet and classifying the sentiments expressed in them as positive, negative or neutral. Sentiment Analyser is of big use for companies and other industries to review their product by knowing what people are thinking about them. It may also come handy to know the mood of writer in a text.

This project is developed by Nishant, Naresh, Gaurav and Krati Under the guiance of Dr. Jaytrilok Chaudhary,as their minor project during their third year of bachelors degree in Computer Science and Engineering.
